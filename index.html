Today's Horse Races

Click on a race time to view speed ratings and generated odds for that race. The odds are calculated based on the horse's ratings and a variety of other factors. Please note that odds may be less reliable for horses that haven't run recently or have limited data.

Column/Number Explanations:

  • Time: The scheduled start time of the race.
  • Track: The name of the racecourse where the race is being held.
  • Weight: The weight (in pounds) carried by the horse during the race.
  • Horse Name: The name of the horse running in the race.
  • L3 AVG: The average speed rating of the horse over its last 3 races.
  • 1LR: The speed rating in the horse’s most recent race (1st last run).
  • 2LR: The speed rating in the horse’s second last race.
  • 3LR: The speed rating in the horse’s third last race.
  • 4LR: The speed rating in the horse’s fourth last race.
  • 5LR: The speed rating in the horse’s fifth last race.
  • 6LR: The speed rating in the horse’s sixth last race.
  • Best: The horse’s best speed rating in its past 6 runs.
  • OR: The Official Rating of the horse.
  • OR/BEST Diff: The difference between the horse’s Official Rating (OR) and its Best speed rating. Don't just back horses blind if the number is positive as this is not profitable long term.
  • LWIN OR: The Official Rating at the time of the horse’s last win.
